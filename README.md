# Rapido Cab Operations & Profit Intelligence

This project analyzes cab trip data to understand:
- Revenue performance
- Demand patterns
- Route profitability
- Business vs personal travel
- Impact of pricing on revenue

## Tools Used
- Google Sheets
- Power BI
- DAX
- Data Modeling
- Time Intelligence


## Dashboard Pages
1. Executive Performance Overview
2. Route & City Profitability Analysis
3. Time & Demand Intelligence

## Key Business Insights
- Identified top revenue generating routes
- Found peak demand hours and days
- Compared business vs personal trip profitability
- Simulated fare increase impact on revenue
